About Me

I'm a Senior at USF majoring in Cybersecurity with a graduation date set to be in the Fall of 2023. I started to want to get into Cybersecurity/IT back when I was younger. I was always the kid who helped his grandparents set up technologies and would be the "call man" for when they needed me to drive over. When in highschool, I was allowed to tour USF and was amazed with the concept of Cybersecurity. From there on out, I had decided that this major was going to be where I wanted to be. I have spent the last few years leading up to graduation falling in love with the space of Cybersecurity and having the pleasure to be able to work on labs/projects relating to Pentesting, Risk Analysis, Cloud Technologies, Red/Bue Team, Threat Assesment, and DevOps to name a few. Moving forward, I am working towards oppurnitnies to learn, and cultivate my skills in a hope that I can use them in the field that I fell in love with.
